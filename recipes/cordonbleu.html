Cheesy cordon bleu

Description

A chicken breast that has been hammered into submission, stuffed with ham of ambiguous origin and a cheese that was simply nearby, then rolled up with the confidence of someone who has watched one YouTube video. Coated in breadcrumbs that were found at the back of the cupboard and fried in oil that was almost hot enough, it emerges golden on one side and pale on the other, leaking a small amount of mystery liquid onto the plate. Served with a lemon wedge that no one will use.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast (emotionally flattened)
  • Ham (of ambiguous origin)
  • Cheese (whichever was nearest)
  • Breadcrumbs (from the back of the cupboard)
  • Oil (almost hot enough)
  • 1 lemon wedge (decorative, do not touch)

Steps

  1. Place chicken breast on a cutting board and hammer it flat while processing unrelated emotions
  2. Lay ham on top — do not think about where it came from
  3. Add cheese. Whichever one was nearest is correct.
  4. Roll the whole thing up with the confidence of someone who has done this once in a dream
  5. Secure with a toothpick and a prayer
  6. Coat in breadcrumbs found at the back of the cupboard, pressing firmly so they stick to about 60% of the surface
  7. Fry in oil that is almost hot enough, achieving gold on one side and pale acceptance on the other
  8. Remove from pan when the mystery liquid begins to escape
  9. Place on plate with lemon wedge at a decorative angle
  10. Do not mention the lemon wedge again
